Experience

UCLA Center for Research in Engineering, Media and Performance (REMAP)


I am currently working with REMAP on an original show based on Amazon Prime Video's show The Man in the High Castle. The show uses AR technology to present stories in parallel timelines simultaneously. As a software developer on the project, I am working on a plugin for the Unreal Engine to receive OSC messages and trigger cues in the game world.

I previously worked on REMAP's Future Storytelling Summer Institute, in which we developed another original show based on the Argentinian novel La Invención de Morel. In this show, we used the Unreal Engine to present a fully virtual performance, streaming live and pre-recorded 2D video of our actors into the 3D game world. For this project, I implemented user interface elements for menus, navigation, and identification of actors in the multiplayer server.
